Learn more about Polish Poetry

The niche works of Polish poets have shaped the general psyche and identity of Poles globally. Their heritage is embedded in the fluidity of style and search for a voice. The beauty and craftmanship of these authors is contagious.

I hope you enjoy discovering the light and shadow of these Poets, as I have. A big 'Thank-You' to the Slavic Language and Literature Department at the University of Michigan for introducing me to these unexpected treasures.
